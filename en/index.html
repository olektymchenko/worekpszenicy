Italian Restaurant in the very center of the city!

Modern decor

Two hundred meters from the Main Square on Krupnicza Street in the tenement house where formerly Jan Matejko's studio was located hides our restaurant.

The smell of pizza encourages you to enter, while the atmospheric music and original interior invites you to meetings in a larger group.

The romantic climate is ensured by a beautiful courtyard garden moved directly from sunny Italy and the magical atmosphere is emphasized by the sound of water from the fountain.

Traditional dishes

With original ingredients

According to the sentence "you can not eat at all but you can not eat badly" we invite you to try dishes based on a simple combination of water and flour.

Our chefs, adding delicious Italian products, will surprise you with their variety of even the most demanding palates!
